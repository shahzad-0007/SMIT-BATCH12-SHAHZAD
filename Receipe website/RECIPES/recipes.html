Recipe 1: Cheesy Tomato Pasta Bake

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound (454 g) dried pasta (e.g., penne, shells)
  • 1 (28-ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Cook pasta according to package directions, drain, and set aside.
  2. In a large saucepan, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add crushed tomatoes, oregano, basil, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Bring to a simmer and cook for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. In a large bowl, combine cooked pasta and tomato sauce. Pour mixture into a greased baking dish.
  4. Top with mozzarella cheese and Parmesan cheese.
  5. Bake for 15-20 minutes, or until cheese is melted and bubbly.

Recipe 2:Easy Chicken Fried Rice

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 pound (454 g) boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cooked and shredded
  • 3 cups cooked rice (cold is preferred)
  • 1 cup frozen mixed vegetables
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped green onions (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in a large wok or skillet over medium-high heat. Add garlic and cook for 30 seconds, until fragrant.
  2. Add shredded chicken and cook for 2-3 minutes, until heated through.
  3. Push chicken to the side of the pan. Add the eggs and scramble them until cooked through.
  4. Stir in cooked rice, frozen vegetables, soy sauce, oyster sauce (if using), salt, and pepper. Cook for 5-7 minutes, or until heated through and vegetables are tender-crisp.
  5. Remove from heat and garnish with chopped green onions.
